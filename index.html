About Us

Driven by the belief that diversity and innovation yield major socio-economic impact, The Student Dream trains the best Black and Latino college students to launch the most innovative, high-impact, startup ventures in the world.

We Produce

The best experiences ever. Our programs, workshops, and events create a community of highly skilled, highly ambitious, and highly diverse entrepreneurs, designers, developers and those who support, sponsor, and advocate for them.

We Offer

Startup Community

We believe that the biggest driver of entrepreneurial success is collaboration. With this in mind we produce meetups, events, and hangouts that create a smart, healthy, and globally diverse startup community.

Training

With an emphasis on experiential education, we produce classes and workshops taught by practitioners and industry leaders that give students the fundamental skills needed to launch and sustain startups.

Accelerator

Starting in Summer 2014, Student Dream will take 10 of the best collegeate minority startups and spend ten weeks giving them the skills, network, and resources needed to build, prototype, fund, launch, and sustain their ventures.

Apprenticeship

Our apprenticeships connect students with seasoned entrepreneurs in the student's industry of focus. Mentors guide students throughout all stages of their businesses development by opening their company's doors to each team.

10 wildly talented teams + 10 high-impact ideas + 24 Empowering Weeks =10 Launched Startups

Impact

There is a goldmine of innovative brilliance that the world is missing out on. As of 2011, Latinos and Blacks founded only 1% of technology startups. Additionally, only 2% and 1% of Latinos and Blacks receive venture capital funding.

This goldmine is hidden in the minds of urban women and students of color who are hungry to bring tech ideas to life, but have yet to be exposed to the inputs needed to do so. We’ve decided to tap into this goldmine and share it with the world. Thus begins the story of The Student Dream; a startup training ground designed to launch early stage tech companies founded by Black, Latin, and Female college students.

By tapping into the innate creativity and intelligence of diverse communities, The Student Dream creates 3 levels of Socio-Economic Impact for Students, Companies, and the World.


FOR STUDENTS

Latino, Black, and Women innovators have a clear and enjoyable path to building the skills, community, and connection needed to launch their innovations.

FOR PRIVATE and PUBLIC SECTORS

Gain access to a New Pipeline of young and diverse tech talent they so desperately need.

FOR GLOBAL COMMUNITIES

Benefit from the jobs and sustainable GDP and jobs that innovation creates.
